##### **My belief system**  
* a belief system is what you think the world is all about  
        + it's what you believe will happen to you after you die 
        + it's what or who you pray to when you are in trouble 

* can be comfortable with your belief system  

* **give you comfort**  
    - you can be comfortable with your belief system  
    - it supports you in times of trouble, answers your questions about your life 
    and what you mean to the universe  
 

##### 自由主义神学  - 基督教的现代主义 
* 《圣经》 只是**个人生活和信仰的指导**， 而非必需遵守及认同所有的权威和教条  

* 自由主义神学的大前提， 是 **以人性的理性分析及科学的发现为依据**
    - 任何与理性和科学不违合的都要反对    
    - 坚信**内心的良知**，这良知可以叫我们在内心，在宇宙间**觉察**上帝
    - 宗教是一种情感体验
